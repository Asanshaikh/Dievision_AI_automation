FAQ

Shopify questions.

Can it answer order status?

Yes, when connected to your store or order system, it can help customers with order status and delivery updates.

Can it handle complaints?

It can collect details and route complaints to a human. It should not argue, overpromise or make policy exceptions without approval.

Does this replace support staff?

No. It reduces repetitive first-response work so staff can focus on sensitive, high-value or complex conversations.
