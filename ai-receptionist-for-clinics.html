FAQ

Clinic questions.

Can it handle medical advice?

No. It should not diagnose or give medical advice. It collects details, answers approved clinic FAQs, books appointments and hands over sensitive cases.

Can it book appointments directly?

Yes, depending on your calendar or booking system integration.

Can it work after clinic hours?

Yes. That is one of the strongest use cases: capturing patient calls when staff is unavailable.
